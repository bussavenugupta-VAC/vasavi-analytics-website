Privacy Policy

Effective: January 2025  ·  vasavianalyticscompany.com/privacy-policy.html

Vasavi Analytics Company ("VAC", "we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of all data entrusted to us by clients, users, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website, products (including VAC ERP), or services.

1. Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: contact details (name, email, phone number), school or organisation information, and enquiry or demo request details. For VAC ERP clients, we process school operational data including student records, attendance, fees, and academic performance — solely on behalf of the school as a data processor.

2. School Data Protection

All school data belongs exclusively to the school. We never sell, share, or use school data for any purpose other than providing the contracted service. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard AES-256 and TLS protocols. Role-based access controls ensure only authorised users can access sensitive information. We undergo regular security reviews to maintain data integrity.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to: provide and improve our services, respond to enquiries and demo requests, send service-related communications, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell personal data to any third party. We do not use school student data for any marketing, analytics, or AI training purposes.

4. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; this may affect certain site features.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable law. School ERP data is retained for the duration of the service contract and deleted within 90 days of contract termination upon written request.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: access personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your personal data, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@vasavianalyticscompany.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

VAC ERP processes student data on behalf of schools as a data processor. Schools are responsible for obtaining appropriate consent under applicable law for student data processed through our platform. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify clients of significant changes via email or a notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
