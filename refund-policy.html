Refund Policy

Effective: January 2025  ·  vasavianalyticscompany.com/refund-policy.html

At Vasavi Analytics Company, we are committed to delivering high-quality software products and services. Because our offerings are custom-developed and service-based in nature, our refund policy reflects the realities of technology engagements.

VAC ERP Subscription

Annual subscriptions may be cancelled within the first 14 days for a full refund. After 14 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable. Monthly subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; no refund is issued for the current billing period already paid. If VAC ERP is unavailable for more than 72 consecutive hours due to our infrastructure failure (excluding scheduled maintenance), a pro-rated service credit will be applied to your next invoice.

Custom Software Development

Work completed and delivered in accordance with the agreed project specification is non-refundable. If a project is cancelled by the client before completion, VAC will retain fees proportional to the work completed and documented at the time of cancellation. Any remaining balance will be refunded within 30 days of written cancellation notice.

Consulting & Analytics Services

Fees paid for consulting sessions, analytics dashboard development, AI implementation, or data engineering engagements are non-refundable once work has commenced. We strongly encourage all clients to clearly define requirements and confirm scope before project initiation to avoid misalignment.

Demo & Trial

Free demo sessions and trial accounts are provided at no cost and are therefore not subject to any refund considerations.

How to Request a Refund

If you believe a refund is warranted due to a service failure on our part, please contact our support team at support@vasavianalyticscompany.com with your order details and a description of the issue. We will review all refund requests fairly and respond within 7 business days.
