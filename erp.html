ERP Modules

Everything your school needs.
In one unified system.

VAC ERP replaces multiple disconnected tools with one integrated platform — saving time, reducing errors, and giving your school complete visibility in real time.

MODULE 01
👨‍🎓
Student Management
Complete student lifecycle — admissions, profiles, class assignments, academic history, document storage, TC generation, and alumni tracking. All searchable and exportable.
MODULE 02
📋
Attendance Management
One-click daily attendance per class. Automatic parent SMS and app alerts for absences. Monthly reports, bulk entry, staff attendance — all in one place.
MODULE 03
🗓️
Timetable Management
Drag-and-drop timetable builder with teacher allocation, period scheduling, substitute teacher management, and holiday calendar integration.
MODULE 04
💳
Fee Management
Flexible fee structures — online and offline payments, automated reminders, digital receipts, concession management, defaulter lists, and detailed collection reports.
MODULE 05
📝
Exam & Result Management
Configurable exam types — unit tests, quarterly, half-yearly, finals. Mark entry by subject, auto-calculated grades, printable report cards, rank lists, and result analytics.
MODULE 06
📱
Parent Mobile App
Real-time access for parents — attendance, exam marks, fee dues, homework, school circulars, and direct teacher messaging. Available on Android and iOS.
MODULE 07
👩‍🏫
Staff Management
Complete HR module — payroll, leave tracking, staff attendance, designations, documents, and performance records for teaching and non-teaching staff.
MODULE 08
🚌
Transport Management
Route planning, bus allocation, student transport assignments, driver details, GPS tracking integration, and route-wise fee collection management.
MODULE 09
📊
Analytics Dashboard
Live school performance dashboards — enrolment trends, fee collection rates, attendance heat-maps, subject-wise performance, and year-on-year comparisons for principals.
MODULE 10
🤖
AI Reports
AI-generated narrative reports — automated progress summaries, at-risk student identification, predictive fee defaulter alerts, and personalised student academic insights.
